Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. Overview

Scoopy That Poopy LLC (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and how you can contact us with questions. This policy applies only to information collected through our website (scoopythatpoopy.com) and direct communication with customers in Las Vegas, Nevada.

2. Information We Collect

When you contact us using our website form, we collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your postal code or address (if provided for quotes)
  • Any message you include

We do not collect payment information through this website. All billing is handled manually through invoices sent directly to customers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your messages and provide service quotes
  • To schedule and confirm service appointments
  • To send invoices or receipts
  • To improve communication and customer service

4. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect or store credit card information, social security numbers, or other sensitive data. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website does not use cookies or tracking pixels for marketing. If we later enable Google Analytics to understand general site traffic, this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Data Retention

We retain customer contact information only as long as necessary to provide services, maintain records, or comply with invoicing requirements.

7. Your Rights

You may request that we delete or update your personal information at any time by emailing scoopythatpoopylv@gmail.com.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to keep customer information secure and prevent unauthorized access. However, no online communication is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our services or practices. The latest version will always be available on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.

10. Contact
