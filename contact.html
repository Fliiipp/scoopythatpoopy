Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We service All of Las Vegas, Nevada! This includes; Centennial Hills, North Las Vegas, Sunrise Manor, Summerlin, Spring Valley, Henderson, and Lake Las Vegas!

How often do you provide services?

We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly recurring services, as well as one-time clean-ups.

What do you do with the pet waste after cleaning it up?

We remove all pet waste from the property and properly dispose of it.

Do I need to be home when my yard is being serviced?

No, you do not need to be home. Just ensure we have yard access and that any aggressive pets are indoors.

Can my dogs be in the yard while it is being serviced?

Yes, as long as you're home and your dog is not aggressive.

What are your prices?

Our prices vary based on; the number of dogs, condition of your yard, and the type of service. Please visit our Services page for detailed pricing information.

What methods of payment do you collect?

We accept cash, check, and all major debit/credit cards.
